Corporate Overview

DIDROV 97 EOOD is a highly dynamic retail enterprise headquartered in Pazardzhik, Bulgaria. Under the leadership of Key Principal Yordan Didrov, we have established a strong presence in the specialized consumer goods market, curating products that inspire creativity and active living.

Our operations span across the Retail Trade sector. We specialize as Miscellaneous Retailers, focusing meticulously on Sporting Goods, Hobby supplies, Musical Instruments, and Books. We believe in providing physical retail excellence backed by robust digital accessibility.

Omnichannel Retail Strategy

To meet the demands of modern consumers, DIDROV 97 EOOD is actively bridging the gap between our brick-and-mortar storefronts and digital commerce. Our goal is to provide a seamless, interconnected shopping experience through our dedicated mobile platforms.

DIDROV Mobile Hub

To provide our customers with a seamless, 24/7 shopping experience, DIDROV 97 EOOD has developed a proprietary e-commerce application published on the Google Play Store.

The DIDROV Mobile Hub transforms how you shop for sporting goods and musical instruments. It integrates our entire catalog into your smartphone, offering secure mobile checkout, exclusive digital loyalty rewards, and AR (Augmented Reality) previews of selected items.

  • Full e-commerce functionality with secure mobile checkout.
  • Digital loyalty card: earn points on every in-app or in-store purchase.
  • Push notifications for exclusive sales, new books, and instrument arrivals.
  • AR viewer to visualize sporting equipment sizes before buying.
