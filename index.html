The Story Behind Our Retail Excellence

A legacy built on quality and dedication.

Founded in Pazardzhik, Bulgaria, DIDROV 97 EOOD has steadily grown into a leading name within the versatile retail sector. Under the visionary leadership of Yordan Didrov, our core mission is to bring exceptional products closer to enthusiasts, hobbyists, and professionals alike. We specialize in curating an extensive, high-quality catalog that ranges from top-tier sporting equipment to fine musical instruments and captivating hobby supplies. Over the years, our unwavering dedication to product authenticity, customer satisfaction, and active community engagement has truly set us apart in a competitive retail market. We fundamentally believe that everyone deserves access to the tools that fuel their passions, and our team works tirelessly every day to source only the best items globally for you.
